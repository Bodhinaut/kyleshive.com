About Kyle

Professionally Trained and Experienced Voice Over Artist.

Kyle Shive is a professionally trained voice actor with a sound that’s grounded, versatile, and real. Whether the script calls for warmth and conversation, compassion and courage, or something rugged, refined, or quirky—Kyle brings heart, storytelling, and a grounded authenticity that resonates.

From relatable commercial reads to standout character work, Kyle delivers with range and presence. He records from a professionally treated home studio equipped with a Neumann microphone, Apollo Solo interface, and Adobe Audition on a 2024 Mac Mini. His audio setup has been vetted and approved by world-class engineers for broadcast-quality sound.

Whatever the tone, whatever the story—Kyle brings it to life. Reach out below to get started.
